On our way home

Just when we had lost all hope we see Biogrid fighting agains lots of little evil gnomes. We rush in to help him. Alice says:

- Take that you meanies. Feel my scratch, meow!

I knock two gnomes out with the tilt of my sword as I make my way towards Biogrid and say victoriously:

- We finally found you!

We kill off the rest of the gnomes quickly and decide to celebrate our victory by going to Jensen's in Kistagallerian.
